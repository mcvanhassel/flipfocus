About FlipFocus

Privacy-first utility apps built with the belief that your data should stay yours.

Our Story

FlipFocus was born from a simple frustration: why should organizing your photos require uploading them to someone else's servers? As a solo developer, I believe in creating tools that respect your privacy and work entirely offline.

Our Mission

We're committed to building privacy-first utility apps that solve real problems without compromising your data. Every FlipFocus app operates 100% offline, ensuring your files never leave your device.

Privacy Commitment

  • 100% Offline: All processing happens locally on your device
  • No Data Collection: We don't track, store, or analyze your usage
  • No Subscriptions: Buy once, own forever with lifetime updates
  • Open Development: Transparent about our practices and roadmap
